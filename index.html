MUSEUM OF CANDY

Welcome to the Museum of Candy! It is here that you can learn about all things delicious and scumptious. From the glazing on a donut to jawbreakers and gumballs, Haza! To get you started on the history of candy, did you know that the ancient Egyptians used a candy like substance to embalm their mummies? Why you ask? Well, the sweetness allowed the dead to more easily enter the afterlife of course!
